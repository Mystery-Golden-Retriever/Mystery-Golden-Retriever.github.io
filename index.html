About Me

Hello, I am Haonan Wang, currently working as a Ph.D. student at Information Sciences Institute (ISI) and ECE department of USC. My research interests primarily focus on deep learning model compression, edge computing, deployment and optimization of deep learning models on edge/cloud systems. I am passionate about exploring new techniques and approaches to improve the performance and efficiency of deep learning models, making them more accessible and practical for real-world applications.

I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from ISI, USC, where my research centered around machine learning, deep learning, and edge/fog/cloud computing. Prior to that, I completed my Master's degree in School of Electronic Science and Engineering, Nanjing University, and my Bachelor's degree in Kuang Yaming Honors School (It was formerly known as the Intensive Class of Science), Nanjing University.
