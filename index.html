About Me

Hello, I am Haonan Wang, currently working as a Research Scientist at ByteDance AI Lab. My research interests primarily focus on deep learning model compression, edge computing, deployment, and optimization of deep learning models on edge/cloud platforms. I am passionate about exploring new techniques and approaches to improve the performance and efficiency of deep learning models, making them more accessible and practical for real-world applications.

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where my research centered around machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision. Prior to that, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Science and Technology of China.

Throughout my academic and professional career, I have published numerous research papers in top-tier conferences and journals. You can find a list of my publications on Google Scholar.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or reach out to me via email for any research collaboration, project opportunities, or general inquiries.
